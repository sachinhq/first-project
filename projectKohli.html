BIOGRAPHY

Virat Kohli (born on 5th November 1988) is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team.He is a right-handed batsman and an occasional medium-fast bowler.He currently represents Royal Challengers Bangluru in the IPL.HEe married to Indian beautiful actoress Anushka Sharma.His nick name is Cheeku.He holds the record of highest run scorer in T20I and IPL.He also holds the record for scoring most centuries in ODI cricket and stands second in the list of most international centuries scored followed by Sachin Tendulkar.
